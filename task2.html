About Varansi (Banarash)

Histriy of Varansi

1. Kashi Vishwanath Temple

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the holiest temples in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is believed that a visit to Varanasi and a prayer at this temple can grant moksha (spiritual liberation). Why It,s Famous: This temple is the center of spiritual life in Varanasi. It attracts millions of pilgrims every year who come to take a dip in the Ganges River and offer prayers to Lord Shiva.

Mankanika Ghat

Known as the primary cremation ghat in Varanasi, it is believed that those who are cremated here attain moksha. The burning pyres can be seen at all times of the day and night.

Manikarnika Ghat is considered one of the most sacred places in the world, where life and death meet. The ghat is deeply connected with Hindu beliefs about the afterlife , and it is an essential part of the spiritual fabric of Varanasi.
